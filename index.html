The Program

Professional Culinary
Training at PVHS

Scratch Cafe is a comprehensive culinary program at Pioneer Valley High School directed by Ms. Montanez. The program operates a fully functional from-scratch kitchen producing restaurant-quality output while developing workforce-ready talent in food preparation, barista services, customer operations, and business management.

Every item served is made entirely from scratch, reinforcing professional kitchen discipline and quality standards that meet industry expectations.
