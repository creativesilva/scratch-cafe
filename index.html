We Are the Panthers.
Everything From Scratch.

Scratch Cafe is an onsite culinary program at Pioneer Valley High School, run by Ms. Montanez. Our students learn real-world skills in food preparation, customer service, and business operations while serving the school community.

Our barista program trains students to craft professional espresso drinks, lattes, and specialty beverages. Every item on our menu is made fresh from scratch.

Run by students. Made with pride.
