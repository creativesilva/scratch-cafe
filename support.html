Why Your Support Matters

Scratch Cafe is more than a kitchen -- it is a professional development pipeline producing workforce-ready talent in culinary arts, barista services, and hospitality management. Your contribution directly funds ingredients, equipment, and program expansion.

Every dollar helps our students gain the hands-on experience they need to build real careers in the food industry.
